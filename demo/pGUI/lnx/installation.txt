Installation of IUP on Linux
============================
You may have luck with apt-get or whatever, it may even be pre-installed.

Otherwise, goto ?? and download the cd, im, and iup binaries
(the steps below are taken from the xxx version, check the LINSTALL files)

mkdir cd
cd cd
tar -xpvzf ../cd-5.9_Linux32_lib.tar.gz
sudo ./install
cd ..

mkdir im
cd im
tar -xpvzf ../im-3.10_Linux32_lib.tar.gz
sudo ./install
cd ..

mkdir iup
cd iup
tar -xpvzf ../iup-3.18_Linux32_lib.tar.gz
sudo ./install
cd ..
