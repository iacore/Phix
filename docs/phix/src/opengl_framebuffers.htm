Frame Buffers

The following routines are wrapped: glGenerateMipmap

The following routines are available but not yet wrapped: glBindFramebuffer, glBindRenderbuffer, glCheckFramebufferStatus, glDeleteFramebuffers, glDeleteRenderbuffers, glFramebufferRenderbuffer, glFramebufferTexture2D, glGenFramebuffers, glGenRenderbuffers, glGetFramebufferAttachmentParameteriv, glGetRenderbufferParameteriv, glIsFramebuffer, glIsRenderbuffer, glRenderbufferStorage

Note that glBlitFramebuffer, glDrawBuffers, glFramebufferParameteri, glFramebufferTextureLayer, glGetFramebufferParameteriv, glInvalidateFramebuffer, glInvalidateSubFramebuffer, glRenderbufferStorageMultisample, and glSampleMaski are not part of OpenGL ES 2.0 or WebGL.
