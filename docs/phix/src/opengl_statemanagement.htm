State Management

The following routines are wrapped: glDepthFunc, glDisable, glEnable, glGet (ie glGetBooleanv, glGetDoublev, glGetFloatv, and glGetIntegerv), glGetError, glHint, and glViewport

The following routines are available but not yet wrapped: glBlendColor, glBlendEquation, glBlendEquationSeparate, glBlendFunc, glBlendFuncSeparate, glColorMask, glCullFace, glDepthMask, glDepthRangef, glFrontFace, glIsEnabled, glLineWidth, glPixelStorei, glPolygonOffset, glSampleCoverage, glScissor, glStencilFunc, glStencilFuncSeparate, glStencilMask, glStencilMaskSeparate, glStencilOp, and glStencilOpSeparate
